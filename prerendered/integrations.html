Integrations

Fits the tools your team already uses

QEPilot lives inside your existing workflow — your tracker, your test framework, your CI. No new platform to adopt, no scripts to maintain.

Issue tracker

Jira

QEPilot installs from the app directory and shows up on tickets like a teammate. It reads acceptance criteria, user stories, and linked docs, then posts manual test cases back onto the ticket for sign-off.

Issue tracker

Linear

Prefer Linear? QEPilot works the same way — point it at an issue and it drafts reviewable test cases in the format your team already uses, right where the work lives.

Automation

Playwright

Approved manual cases become real, runnable Playwright specs — not pseudocode or suggestions. Every spec maps back to the acceptance criterion it verifies.

Automation

Playwright-MCP

QEPilot drives Playwright through Playwright-MCP, so it authors and validates specs the same way a human engineer would, then hands them to your suite.

Pipeline

Your CI

Generated specs wire directly into your existing CI (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, CircleCI, and more), running on every pull request — nothing new to host or maintain.

Grounding

Your domain knowledge

Link product specs, prior tickets, and design docs. QEPilot retrieves them with RAG so each test reflects how your product actually behaves — not a generic guess.
